# AdaSci Agentic AI for Leaders Bootcamp 2025

Build production-ready agents, orchestrate multi-agent systems and deploy them with confidence.

This intensive, hands-on program from ADaSci equips professionals and leaders to design, develop, and deploy agentic AI systems that create measurable business value.

Over five immersive days, participants learn the full stack, from LangChain, LlamaIndex, and CrewAI to observability, safety, and cloud deployment through a structured blend of live sessions, guided labs, and strategic leadership discussions.

Walk away with real working systems, reusable frameworks, and a strategic roadmap for integrating agentic AI into your organisation.
